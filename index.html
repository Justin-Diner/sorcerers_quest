How to Play:

  1. Press the A and D Keys to move left and right. Press spacebar to jump.
  2. Save the townspeople by breaking down the castle walls with your spell!
  3. Press C to cast your spell.
  4. You can only cast your spell once every 3 seconds.
  5. Avoid the guard's arrows. Three shots and you lose!
